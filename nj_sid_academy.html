ABOUT NJ SID ACADEMY

Welcome to the New Jersey SID Academy

The New Jersey SID Academy is a comprehensive educational platform and consulting resource designed to help municipalities, downtown organizations, and property owners successfully create and launch Special Improvement Districts across the state.

We combine Storefront Mastery's 20 years of hands-on experience in economic development with modern technology and AI-powered tools to streamline the complex process of establishing a SID — from initial feasibility assessment through ordinance adoption and long-term operations.

Our approach leverages data-driven analysis, interactive planning tools, and a top-tier network of municipal attorneys, planners, and economic development practitioners to help communities move efficiently toward their local economic development goals.
